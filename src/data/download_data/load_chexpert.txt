You can download the small version (11 GB) of CheXpert, downloaded from kaggle https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ashery/chexpert.

For the original dataset (420 GB) go to the bottom of the CheXpert webpage https://stanfordmlgroup.github.io/competitions/chexpert/. You need to register and fill out a form to accept the licence. Then you get a link to download the dataset with AzCopy (https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/storage/common/storage-use-azcopy-v10).
